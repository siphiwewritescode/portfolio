Concept

ChowRun Takeaways wants to improve its understanding of order fulfillment and delivery performance. The goal is to track delivery times, identify peak ordering periods, and analyze the performance of various restaurants and delivery teams. This analysis helps ensure customer satisfaction and pinpoint areas needing improvement, such as delivery efficiency and order management.

Implementation

To create this report, I followed these steps:

Data Preparation:

I worked with data from the month of October 2021, which included details about orders (order date, order time, order number), fulfillment status (fulfilled by, shipping address suburb), and delivery details (order status, delivery times, assigned delivery drivers). I ensured that the data was clean, accurate, and properly formatted for analysis.

Designing the Report:

I designed the report with the following components:

  1. Cards:

    • Total Revenue: Displayed the total revenue for the month.

    • Total Delivered Orders: Showed the total number of orders successfully delivered.

    • Average Order Duration: Provided the average time it took for orders to be delivered.

    • Ordering Peak Time: Displayed the time period during which the highest number of orders were placed.

    • Date With Most Orders: Highlighted the day with the highest volume of orders.

  2. Slicers:

    • Delivery Team: Allowed users to filter data based on the delivery team.

    • Time of Day: Allowed users to view order data for specific times of the day.

  3. Visuals:

    • Total Sales by Restaurant Name (Stacked Bar Chart): Displayed total sales per restaurant, broken down by the order status.

    • Number of Orders by Restaurant and Order Delivery Status (Stacked Column Chart): Showed the number of orders per restaurant, grouped by delivery status.

    • Total Deliveries by Delivery Team (Pie Chart): Illustrated how deliveries were distributed among different delivery teams.

    • Total Orders by Top 5 Suburbs (Clustered Column Chart): Displayed the number of orders per suburb, highlighting the top 5 suburbs.

Insights Discovered

Total Revenue and Delivered Orders:

The total revenue and the total number of delivered orders helped identify the overall sales performance and customer fulfillment rate.

Average Order Duration:

The average time for orders to be delivered showed areas for potential improvement in delivery speed. In some cases, long delivery times could indicate delays in order fulfillment or delivery team performance.

Peak Ordering Time:

The peak ordering time highlighted the busiest times of day for orders. This insight could help optimize staffing and ensure adequate delivery resources during high-demand periods.

Date With Most Orders:

The day with the most orders was identified, allowing the team to understand seasonal or time-specific trends. This information could be useful for planning promotions or resource allocation.

Orders by Restaurant and Order Delivery Status:

This analysis showed how different restaurants performed in terms of order fulfillment and delivery status. Some restaurants may have a higher proportion of orders that are delayed or unfulfilled, signaling potential issues in their operations.

Delivery Team Performance:

The analysis of total deliveries by each delivery team showed which teams had the highest number of successful deliveries. It also revealed areas where certain teams might need additional support or training to improve performance.

Orders by Suburb:

The clustered column chart displayed the most frequent delivery locations, highlighting the top 5 suburbs. Understanding the geographical spread of orders helped the team identify areas where delivery strategies could be optimized or adjusted.
